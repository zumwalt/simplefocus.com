The story of how the plugin came to be.

Images

Late one afternoon, Casey (@zumwalt) was working on a website design for a Simple Focus client. Casey was using large images that were designed in photoshop inside a simple fluid layout for a faux in-browser expreience. After a few hours of working, he asked John (@jhnwlsn) to come give some creative feedback.

As the conversation between John and Casey grew, they both noticed that they were fond of how the images/content remained proportional and grew with the size of the window. Casey mentioned that "this could be done with some simple math," but the conversation ended there and they both returned to their work.

Thoughts

Over the next week, John decided to give it a shot after it had been sitting in his idea cooker for about a week. Within one-hour, he had a full prototype that would change text based on window width.
